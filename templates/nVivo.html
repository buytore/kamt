NVivo

Applicability

NVivo is software used to support qualitative and mixed methods research and reporting through providing a platform to organize the sources of unstructured or semi-structured text based data that form the basis of a large scale research project or qualitative analysis. Use the software to, store links to the sources; organize the content into themes identified during the analysis; and to illustrate links where themes appear in more than one of the sources. NVivo can be used by individual or teams to take notes; map the flow of the project; or generate queries to identify the relationship between qualitative themes and the quantitative data in a mix method analysis. Use NVivo to compose a report detailing findings; run queries to report on and export illustrations of query results.
