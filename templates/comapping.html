Solution or Tool: Comapping

Summary

Comapping is online mind mapping software to manage and share information. Use it to take notes, plan and organize. It's a way to have one set of notes. One set of files. For everyone.
Map out your work. Map out your life. Become a Comapper.

Characteristics of Comapping

Better way to take notes

  • One set of notes & files for you and your team.
  • Attach any file format to your notes.
  • Put all of your notes in one place.
  • Quickly share important information with your team, clients or suppliers.
  • Great for Meetings, Seminars, and Brainstorming.
